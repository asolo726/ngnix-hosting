About Me

I grew up on the south side of Chicago. Wasn't cut out for sports so I looked towards technology. I'm not too sure what I'll end up doing. Might be creating video games or something else that's tech related. But what I can be sure of is that it'll help others create wonderful memories with each other. That's the feeling I want to able to spread. Gaming allowed me to share to memories. So I want to give it back.
